Our Story

Cilza Cakes Studio, baked in small batches.

Cilza Cakes Studio started with a simple question: “Can a cake feel good after the last slice?” We loved the look of celebration cakes, but not the heavy, sleepy feeling that often came afterwards.

Today our bakers switch on the ovens every morning in a small London kitchen, whisking and folding in small batches. We use good cocoa, whole-grain flours where it makes sense and real fruit for colour.

  • Recipes developed to be lighter and more balanced - not sugar bombs.
  • Options for less sugar, vegan and nut-free recipes where clearly marked.
  • Every cake is finished by hand with a quick visual check before delivery.

Whether it's a quiet tea break, a big milestone or the office's Friday treat table, our goal is simple: cakes that look beautiful, taste indulgent and still feel kind to your day.
